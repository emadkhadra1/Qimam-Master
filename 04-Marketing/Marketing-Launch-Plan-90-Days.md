# Qimam Technology - 90-Day Marketing Launch Plan

## Purpose

This document defines the first 90-day marketing execution plan for Qimam Technology.

The goal is not to create noise. The goal is to generate qualified leads, book demos, and convert early customers for the four active solutions.

## Active Solutions

Marketing in this phase will focus only on:

1. Qimam Construction ERP
2. Qimam Real Estate ERP
3. Qimam HR & Payroll
4. Qimam 3PL & Logistics

Unfinished products must not be promoted as ready solutions.

---

## Strategic Focus

### Primary Market
Saudi Arabia

### Secondary Markets
GCC and Egypt

### Primary Segment
Construction companies

### Secondary Segments
- Real estate developers
- 3PL and logistics operators
- Medium businesses needing HR and payroll automation

---

## Core Marketing Message

Qimam Technology helps companies move from manual operations, scattered tools, and disconnected spreadsheets to cloud-based, industry-ready ERP solutions that provide better control, faster decisions, and scalable growth.

---

## Positioning

Qimam Technology is not positioned as an Odoo implementation company.

Qimam Technology is positioned as an industry-focused business technology company delivering ERP, cloud, automation, and AI-enabled solutions.

---

## 90-Day Goals

By the end of 90 days, the target is to achieve:

- 60 professional social media posts
- 4 solution brochures
- 1 company profile
- 1 sales deck
- 1 WhatsApp brochure
- 100 targeted LinkedIn connections
- 40 qualified conversations
- 20 demo requests
- 10 proposals
- 3 signed customers

---

## Month 1 - Foundation and Trust

### Objective
Build market presence, sales materials, and credibility.

### Deliverables
- Finalize company positioning
- Finalize website messaging
- Publish company profile
- Publish WhatsApp brochure
- Prepare sales deck
- Create LinkedIn company page
- Create Facebook page
- Create X profile
- Prepare 20 posts
- Prepare demo request flow

### Main Content Themes
- ERP mistakes in companies
- Construction project cost control
- Why Excel is not enough
- Cloud ERP benefits
- AI in business operations
- HR automation
- Real estate sales control
- 3PL settlement challenges

---

## Month 2 - Lead Generation

### Objective
Start direct outreach and collect qualified leads.

### Activities
- LinkedIn outreach to CEOs, CFOs, COOs, Project Directors
- WhatsApp brochure sharing in business groups
- Email outreach to construction and real estate companies
- Publish weekly educational posts
- Start collecting demo requests
- Prepare first webinar concept

### Lead Magnets
- ERP Readiness Checklist
- Construction Cost Control Guide
- HR Payroll Automation Checklist
- Real Estate ERP Selection Guide

---

## Month 3 - Conversion and Sales Pipeline

### Objective
Convert conversations into demos, proposals, and signed deals.

### Activities
- Run targeted campaigns for Construction ERP
- Follow up on all warm leads
- Run product demos
- Send proposals
- Build objections handling sheet
- Prepare first customer onboarding workflow

### First Paid Campaign Focus
Qimam Construction ERP

### Campaign Message
Control projects, contracts, costs, subcontractors, and cash flow from one cloud-based ERP platform.

---

## Marketing Channels

### LinkedIn
Primary channel for B2B authority and outreach.

### WhatsApp
Primary channel for early distribution and direct relationship marketing.

### Website
Conversion hub for demo requests and solution explanation.

### Email
Professional follow-up and proposal nurturing.

### Google Ads
To be used after website conversion flow is ready.

### Meta Ads
To be used for retargeting and awareness, not primary acquisition at first.

## Immediate Priority

The first product to market aggressively is:

Qimam Construction ERP

Reason:
- High contract value
- Clear business pain
- Strong need for cost control
- Easier executive-level messaging
- Strong fit for Saudi market

---

## Execution Principle

Focus beats expansion.

Qimam must first become known for solving real operational problems in four clear sectors before expanding into more products.
